THE PROBLEM

Skilled Youth.
No Market Access.

Bangladesh produces 700,000 to 900,000 graduates annually. Most enter a labor market that is completely alien to their academic training, undervalues their skills, and drains the life out of them until they give up on their dreams.

The gap between income potential and training is not a resource gap — it's a system gap. youth have capability. What they lack is structure, mentorship, and a legitimate entry point into the global digital market.

THE SOLUTION

A Micro-Agency
That Runs On
young Talent.

Digital Enterprise Lab embeds youth into functional service-delivery structures. They don't just learn, they work. They don't just graduate, they earn.

DEL turns skill-acquisition into real market experience by running live client projects through youth-powered micro-agencies — with defined roles, revenue sharing, and actual client accountability.

HOW IT WORKS

The Micro-Agency
Model.

DEL operates through a structured four-tier system. Every participant has a defined role, a clear progression path, and a stake in real revenue-generating work. No simulation. No hypothetical. Real clients, real deliverables, real income.

01 36 per cohort

Lead Generator

The entry point. Lead Generators identify and qualify potential clients, learn sales fundamentals, and feed the pipeline. No prior technical experience required — just drive and communication instinct.

  • Outbound client outreach
  • Market prospecting
  • Communication training
  • Pipeline management basics
→
02 18 per cohort

Apprentice

Apprentices learn a chosen digital skill directly under expert supervision. They build portfolio-grade work in a team environment — not academic exercises. Mentored guidance at every step until skill is mastered.

  • Hands-on skill learning
  • Mentored skill development
  • Portfolio-grade outputs
  • Client-facing exposure
→
03 9 per cohort

Service Provider

Experienced practitioners who directly contribute to client work, manage quality, and reports to micro-agency lead. They operate as billable professionals within our structure.

  • Real project execution
  • Quality assurance
  • Apprentice overseeing
  • Client feedback tracking
→
04 3 per cohort

Digital Nomad

The apex of the DEL pathway. Digital Nomads run the micro-agency, manage client portfolios, and set the strategic direction. They are, in practice, youth founders operating a real business.

  • Micro-agency leadership
  • Full client portfolio management
  • Revenue responsibility
  • Team development

ABOUT DEL

Built for the
Serious youth.

Digital Enterprise Lab was founded on a simple but urgent observation: Bangladesh's youth have the capability to compete in the global digital economy. What they lack is the structure, mentorship, and market access to do it.

DEL is not a club. It is not a workshop series. It is not a certificate program. It is a working institution — a youth-led entity that generates real revenue by delivering real services to real clients.

Every element of DEL — the roles, the micro-agency structure, the service departments — is designed to produce one outcome: a youth who is market-ready from day one.

MISSION

To build Bangladesh's first youth-powered service-export ecosystem — turning digital skills into consistent, scalable income for youth while delivering quality services to global clients.

VISION

A Bangladesh where every skilled youth has a clear, structured pathway from campus to global market — through practice, mentorship, and real work.
